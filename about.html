About Us

JMD Fabricators is a trusted manufacturer of Scaffolding, Shuttering / Staging & Aluminum Tower Products

JMD Fabricators is a leading manufacturing company specializing in high-quality Scaffolding systems, Shuttering / Staging products, and Aluminum Towers for the construction and infrastructure industry. Based in Gurgaon, we are committed to delivering durable, safe, and performance-driven products that meet industry standards.

With years of manufacturing expertise, we focus on precision engineering, superior raw materials, and strict quality control processes to ensure reliable and long-lasting products. Our solutions are designed to support a wide range of construction applications, from residential and commercial projects to large-scale infrastructure developments.

At JMD Fabricators, safety and quality are at the core of everything we do. Each product undergoes rigorous testing to ensure strength, load-bearing capacity, and compliance with safety norms. We continuously invest in modern manufacturing techniques to improve efficiency and product performance.

Our experienced team of engineers, technicians, and skilled workers is dedicated to delivering consistent quality and timely production. We work closely with builders, contractors, and project managers to understand their requirements and provide customized fabrication solutions.

Reliability and customer satisfaction are the foundation of our business. We take pride in our ability to deliver products on time without compromising on quality. With a strong manufacturing setup and efficient processes, we are capable of handling projects of varying scales and complexities.

We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients by offering dependable products, transparent communication, and consistent support. Our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation has made JMD Fabricators a preferred manufacturing partner in the construction industry.

In conclusion, JMD Fabricators stands as a reliable and professional manufacturer of Scaffolding, Shuttering / Staging, and Aluminum Tower products. With a focus on precision manufacturing, safety compliance, and customer satisfaction, we continue to support construction projects with solutions that deliver strength, stability, and long-term value.
